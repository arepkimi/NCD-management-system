Health Recommendations for Diabetes

Here are your personalized recommendations for managing your diabetes level!

Recommended Diet

  • Replace white rice with brown rice or quinoa to help manage blood sugar levels.
  • Consume non-starchy vegetables such as broccoli, spinach and kale to improve insulin sensitivity.
  • Include healthy fats like avocado and olive oil to regulate blood sugar and prevent spikes.
  • Avoid sugary drinks like soda and fruit juices. Opt for water or unsweetened herbal teas.
  • Limit the intake of refined carbohydrates, such as bread and pastries and choose whole grains instead.

Recommended Exercise

  • Engage in moderate aerobic exercises, such as walking or swimming, for at least 150 minutes per week.
  • Incorporate strength training exercises to help maintain muscle mass and regulate blood sugar levels.
  • Try yoga or stretching exercises to improve flexibility and reduce stress, which can affect blood sugar control.
  • Take short breaks during long periods of sitting to increase circulation and avoid blood sugar spikes.

Recommended Lifestyle Changes

  • Monitor your blood glucose regularly to understand how different foods and activities affect your levels.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day, especially if you experience frequent urination.
  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule, as poor sleep can negatively impact insulin sensitivity.
  • Practice stress-reduction techniques like deep breathing or meditation, as stress can elevate blood sugar levels.
  • Quit smoking and limit alcohol consumption to reduce the risk of complications associated with diabetes.
